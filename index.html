ttTools
Turntable Tools

A collection of quality of life tools for turntable.fm.
To install: drag the links to your bookmarks bar.
For more help with automatic installing, visit the wiki.
Boost Mode (WIP)
Perfect for low end pcs.
Disables: Avatars, Video and Animations
Auto Dark Mode
If you're someone who perfers day at day and night at night here's an automatic dark/light theme for you.
Dark Mode
If you want the night 24/7 here's a darkmode bookmarklet for you.
Work Mode
Removes all distracting things like avatars, video and replaces the stage background with a duller image.
Hide ttStats
Hide header bars & search bar for ttStats.
Hide Thompsn Stats
Hide the search bar on Thompsn.
Custom Mentions
Use custom mention sounds when you get mentioned.
Default: plug.dj badoop. For more customisation, use the userscript version included inside of the GitHub repo.
Custom Mention/Action Colours
Change the colour of mentions and actions (user played, user was booted, etc).
Resize Chat
Allows the chat to be resized and has options to increase and decrease the chat's text size on the fly.
Please note: these are a work in progress and are subject to change.
